Sri Lanka Cricket Museum



The Sri Lanka Museum' house some of the most iconictrasures of Sri Lanka Cricket depicting its incredible cricketing journey,which goes back to over a century.The museum proudly narrates Sri Lanka Cricket's courageous journey towards becoming a test nation, its achievements made during the Pre and Post Test era,the country's stupendous records achieved in the limited overs arena.

Sri Lanka National Cricket Team Players



The national cricket team represents Sri Lanka in international matches.Cricket is the most popular sport in Sri Lanka.The Sri Lankan team transformed themselves from the underdog status to a leading cricketing nation during the 1990s.

Sri Lankan Cricket Grounds.




The main cricket ground in Sri Lanka is R.Premadasa stadium in Colombo. It's one of the largest and most frequently used cricket stadiums in the country,hosting various international and domestic matches. Also there are some several grounds like: Pallekale Stadium,Kandy Galle Stadium,Galle and Dambulla Stadium,Dambulla
