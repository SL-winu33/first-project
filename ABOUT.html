How Do You Apply For Application To Cricket Association

In order to become a proffessional player you must have experince playing for a cricket club. If you are not currently a member of a club, find a local club to join to a game experince.Once you are a member, you can be nominated for trials into stay borough teams, minor country teams, then country team.

Conditions of Enrolment.

  1. Currently accredited Level One Cricket Coah with minimum 3 years as a practising Level One Coach.
  2. Valid first aid course certification.
  3. Recently issued Police Clearance Certificate (Not more than six months old)
  4. Attendence to SLC Coach Education Programs.
  5. Condition of Health-Physically fit and mentaly sound.
  6. Valid Coaching License.
  7. Sound knowledge of Cricket.
